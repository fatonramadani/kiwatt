# Kiwatt Marketing Plan 2026

## Executive Summary

Kiwatt is a SaaS platform for managing Local Energy Communities (CEL/LEC) in Switzerland. Currently in private alpha, our goal is to acquire **50 paying customers** by end of 2026 through targeted outreach to energy community managers, municipalities, and cooperatives.

---

## 1. Market Analysis

### Target Market Size (Switzerland)
- **~1,500** existing LECs in Switzerland (2024)
- **~500** new LECs formed annually
- **Total Addressable Market**: ~2,000 active LECs
- **Serviceable Market**: ~500 LECs (>10 members, need automation)

### Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

| Segment | Description | Monthly Volume | Priority |
|---------|-------------|----------------|----------|
| **Municipal LECs** | Communes managing citizen energy communities | 100-200k kWh | HIGH |
| **Energy Cooperatives** | Established cooperatives (Groupe-E, Romande Energie partners) | 50-100k kWh | HIGH |
| **Neighborhood LECs** | Private initiatives with 10-30 members | 20-50k kWh | MEDIUM |
| **Property Managers** | Régies managing multi-building LECs | 50-150k kWh | MEDIUM |

### Competitive Landscape

| Competitor | Positioning | Price | Weakness |
|------------|-------------|-------|----------|
| **Spreadsheets** | DIY solution | Free | Time-consuming, error-prone |
| **Monétel** | Telecom-backed | Unknown | Complex, enterprise-focused |
| **Clever Energy** | Full-service provider | High | Expensive, not self-service |
| **Kiwatt** | Self-service SaaS | CHF 0.01/kWh | New, alpha stage |

[Signature]
